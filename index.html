About Me
Web development Is My Passion

Well i’ve been a Web developer for 10 years, and i feel that i know less now than when i first started. And its perfectly normal! Because the more you learn the more you know, and the more you know the wiser you become. And a wise man knows that he knows nothing, and in consequence try to learn more! I personally have never met a great developer that claimed to know everything. Usually only beginners do that. God knows i had my fair share of delusion of grandeur. Its really easy to mistake arrogance with knowledge when we first start. But back on point! A good way to overcome this is to focus your efforts. "The art of being wise is knowing what to overlook".
